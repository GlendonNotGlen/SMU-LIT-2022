Problem Statement
Project Management Tool

When a non-legally trained individual consults lawyers, they are often not aware of the legal issues in play â€“ at the same time, legal departments are increasingly required to be more agile. Build a project management tool that allows non-legally trained individuals to submit work requests to lawyers, while being easily usable by a legal team and adopting features which are tailored for legal use.

Our Solution
Jura

A user-friendly, mobile-responsive web application. Jura seeks to improve the workflow of in-house lawyers who receive requests from non-legally trained employees. Equiped with features such as sorting, prioritisation and case allocation, Jura was developed with the needs of the in-house lawyers in mind. At the same time, as a requestor, creating new requests or viewing previously submitted requests are made effortless through Jura. Try out our solution below!
